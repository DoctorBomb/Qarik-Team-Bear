policies, as a result of which the Borrower: (i) purchased for cash
the equivalent of approximately US$798,300,000 of Eligible Debt (as
hereinafter defined) with a discount of 74.81252; (ii) issued Front
Loaded Interest Reduction Bonds due 2012 (the “FLIRBs") in exchange
for the equivalent of approximately US$1,657,500,000 of Eligible
Debt (as hereinafter rear (iii) issued Discount Bonds due 2024
(the "Discount Bonds") | exchange for the equivalent of

for cash the equivalent of approximately US$223,300,000 of PDI on
Eligible Debt (as hereinafter defined); and issued PDI Bonds in
exchange for the equivalent of US$1,614,000 in PDI on Eligible Debt
(as hereinafter defined) ; :

(B) the Borrower has requested the assistance of the Bank

of the cost of: (i) the purchase price of Eligible Debt (as

ARTICLE I
General Conditions; Definitions

Section 1.01. The "General Conditions Applicable to Loan and
Guarantee Agreements" of the Bank, dated January 1, 1985, with the
modifications set forth in Schedule 4 to this Agreement (the General
Conditions), constitute an integral part of this Agreement.

Section 1.02. Unless the context otherwise requires, the
several terms defined in the General Conditions and in the Preamble
to this Agreement have the respective meanings therein set forth and
the following additional terms have the following meanings:

(a) "Debt Purchase Offer" means the debt purchase offer made |

by the Borrower under the agreements entered into pursuant to the

1994 Financing Plan;

(b) “Eligible Debt" has the same meaning ascribed thereto

in the 1994 Financing Plan; |

(c) "Fiscal Year" means the fiscal year of the Borrower,
which begins on January 1 and ends on December 31 of each year;

(d) "Participating Lenders" means the commercial banks and
other financial institutions with Eligible Debt that have
participated in the 1994 Financing Plan; and |

(e) "Purchase" means the aggregate of cash payments made by
the Borrower to Participating Lenders under the agreements entered
into pursuant to the Debt Purchase Offer and to be partially
financed out of the proceeds of the Loan; and "Specific Purchase"
means the specific portions of the Purchase to be financed out of
the proceeds of the Loan in accordance with Schedule 1 to this

Agreement.
