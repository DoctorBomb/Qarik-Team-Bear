SCHEDULE1
Program Deserqyhon
The objective of the Program is to increase access to improved water Supply
services in Pcmi-urban Areas in Uttarakhand.
The Program consists of die pen-urban water supply activities set fortli in the
Program implementing Entity's Water Supply and Sanitation Program, inckiding: (a)
construction, rehabilitation, augmentation and/or expansion of water supply piped
networks and metered services connections in pen-urban arra; (b) stren9diening of
policies on, and planning and monitoring of, water supply services in peri-urban area,
including: (i) development and implementatim of a service-oriented water supply sector
policy for peri-urban area ; (ii) stmitgdming of misting monitoring and evaluation
systems on water service performance in pen-urban areas; and (di) preparation and
adoption of water supply master plans for pen-urban areas; and (c) provision of technical
assistance to: (i) manage the Program; (ii) strengthen Program Implementing Entity's
governance and accountability, (W) carry out technical assessments and sector studies on
water supply services in peri-urban services; and Ov) build capacity and provide
professionalization on water supply.

APPENDIX
Section I. Definitions
1. 
"Anti-corruption Guidelines" means, for purposes of paragraph 5 of the Appendix
to the General Conditions, the Bank's "Guidelines on Preventing and Combating
Fraud and Corruption in Program-for-Results Financing." dated February 1, 2012,
and revised July 10, 2015.
2. 
"Approved Master Plan" means, for purposes of DL1#5, the annual master plan for
water supply in Pen-urban Areas approved by DDWS, which has met all the
requirements set forth in the POM.
3. 
"Category" means a category set forth in the table in Section IV.A.2 of Schedule
2 to this Agreement
4. 
"Disbursement Linked Indicator" or "DI" means in respect of a given Category,
the indicator related to said Category as set forth in the table in Section IV.A.2 of
Schedule 2 to this Agreement.
5. 
"Disbursement Linked Result" or "DLR" means in respect of a given Category,
the result under said Category as set forth in Schedule 4 to this Agreement, based
on the achievement of which the amount of the Loan allocated to said result may
be withdrawn in accordance with the provisions of said Section IV.
6. 
"DDWS" means the Program Implementing Entity's Department of Drinking
Water Sanitation.
7. 
"General Conditions" means the "International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development General Conditions for IBRD Financing, Program-for-Results
Financing", dated July 14, 2017.
8. 
"Improved Water Supply Service" means, for purposes of DLI#1, the water supply
service provided by an Operator to users within a selected Pen-urban Area, which
has met all die requirements set forth in the POM, including the provision of water
supply services for a minimum period of sixty (16) hours in one (1) day over the
course of one Year.
9. 
"M&E" means monitoring and evaluation.
10. 
"Operators" means, collectively, UJS and UJN, the Program Implementing
Entity's water institutions who have signed Performance Agreements, or another
operator proposed by the Borrower and the Program Implementing Entity, with
respect to which the Bank has assessed and confirmed in writing to the Borrower
- 15 
and the Project Implementing Entity that it is an acceptable operator for the
purpose of this Agreement.
11. 
"Performance Agreement" means the agreement to be signed between the Program
Implementing Entity and an Operator, under the terms and conditions acceptable
to the bank, as the same may be amended from time to time with the agreement of
the Bank.
12. 
"Per-urban Area" means the Project Implementing Entity's pen-urban area
acceptable to the Bank, which has been selected after meeting all the requirements
set forth in the POM.
13. 
"Program Agreement" means the agreement between the Bank and the Program
Implementing Entity relating to the implementation of the Program, as such
agreement may be amended from time to time.
14. 
"Program Operations Manual" and the acronym "POM" means the Program
Implementation Entity's manual acceptable to the Bank dated November 6, 2017,
and referred to in Section C of the Schedule to the Program Agreement, setting
out, inter alia, the objectives, components, implementation arrangements and
verification protocols for the Program as may be amended from time to time with
the agreement of the Bank.
15. 
"Program Action Plan" means the Program Implementation Entity's plan dated
November 6, 2017, included in the POM (referred to in Section C of the Schedule
to the Program Agreement), as may be amended from time to time in accordance
with the provisions of Section C of the Schedule to the Program Agreement
16. 
"Program Implementing Entity" means the Borrower's State of Uttarakhand.
17. 
"Signature Date" means the later of the two dates on which the Borrower and the
Bank signed this Agreement and such definition applies to all references to "the
date of the Loan Agreement" in the General Conditions.
18. 
"Sustainability of Water Supply Service Delivery " means, for purposes of DL#2,
the water supply system administrated by an Operator within a selected Peri-urban
Area, which has met all the requirements set forth in the POM, including the
following four (4) requirements (i.e. TSIS units): (a) the existence of a
Performance Agreement; (b) 100% of its water supply connections have metering
systems in place; (c) the operation and maintenance costs for the water supply
system are secured; and (d) at least 70% of sampled customers is satisfied with the
Improved Water Supply Service.

"TSIS" means the Total System Improvement Score, a numeric value equivalent

"UJN" means the UftarakhandJalNigam.
21. 
"UJS" means the Unarakhand Jal Sansthan.
22. 
"Water Supply and Sanitation Program" means the Program Implementing
Entity's mid-term water supply and sanitation program for the period 2018/2024,
approved by the Government Order No. 1369/XXDC(2)/17-2(36Pey.)/2012 of
September 7, 2017, which consists of six (6) sub sectorial components: (a) urban
water supply; (b) urban sanitation; (c) rural water supply; (d) rural sanitation; (e)
peri-urban water supply (i.e. the Program); and (f) peri-urban sanitation.

"Year" means Year 1, Year 2, Year 3, Year 4, Year 5 or Year 6.
24.

28.
